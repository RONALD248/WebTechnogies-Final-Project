Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings, I recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance. Portrait sessions can typically be scheduled 2-4 days ahead. So that I can be able to plan well so that we make our things in order

What is included in the pricing?

All packages include my time, talent, professional equipment, basic editing, and digital images. Prints and albums are additional.

Do you travel for photoshoots?

Yes! Travel within 50 kilometers is included. Beyond that, reasonable travel fees apply.

How long until I receive my photos?

Portrait sessions: 2 days. Weddings 1 weeks. Rush editing is available for an additional fee.
